About Us

We're three students who have seen the negative impacts of this pandemic on small, local businesses. Minority-owned businesses have been disproportionately affected and we wanted to bring our knowledge and passion for tech to support them.

We created BuyPOC for UOttaHacks as a platform to amplify the reach of smaller businesses in Ottawa, as well as give like-minded consumers a resource to support local by purchasing their products and services.
